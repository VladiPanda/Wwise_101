AK::SpatialAudio::SetGeometryInstance or AK::SpatialAudio::SetRoom: Geometry instances which are not watertight cannot be used for the definition of a room.

A geometry instance used for the definition of a room must be watertight.

推荐的解决步骤：

  • Check that your geometry is watertight (does not contain any holes) before using it for the definition of a room.
