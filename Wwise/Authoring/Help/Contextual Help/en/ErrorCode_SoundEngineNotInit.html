Sound Engine is not initialized yet or Init bank has not been loaded. Function: ...

Most functions cannot be called before the Sound Engine is completely initialized through a call to AK::SoundEngine::Init. Many functions will have no effect until the Init bank is completely loaded.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Change the order of function calls so the specified function is called after AK::SoundEngine::Init and the loading of the Init.bnk file.

  • Use the callback functionality of the AK::SoundEngine::LoadBank function to know when the Init.bnk file is finished loading, and make the call after.

  • Use AK::SoundEngine::RegisterAudioDeviceStatusCallback to know when the first Audio Device is ready, then make the call after.
