Rescheduling music clips because a track was modified.

This happens when the a track is modified while playing. Usually occurs when Wwise is connected to the game. It caused the sequencer to reschedule clips which may cause music desynchronization.

Probable causes:

  • The music structure was modified while it played.

  • The in-game music structure is different than the one in the Wwise project (old content)

Recommended resolution step:

  • Regenerate your music SoundBanks and redeploy.
