Cannot schedule music switch transition in upcoming segments: using Exit Cue.

This error occurs when a Music Switch container is incapable of scheduling a music transition to honor the prescribed transition rule. This can happen, for example, when the playlist corresponding to the current state is made up of very small segments, and the rule asks for a transition point very far in the future, because of a very large fade in / out or destination pre-entry. Playlists may never schedule more than 64 segments ahead of time.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Adjust fade times.

  • Adjust pre-entry times.

  • Reduce Music Switch container complexity.
