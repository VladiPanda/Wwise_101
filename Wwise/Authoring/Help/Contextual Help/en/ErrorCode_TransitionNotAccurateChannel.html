Transition not sample-accurate due to mixed channel configurations.

This error occurs when two sounds with different channel configuration are set up to play one after the other in a sample-accurate transition. Such transitions can exist in Random/Sequence Containers, in Interactive Music, and when using the Dynamic Sequence mechanism in code. The sound will be stopped.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Identify the sounds involved and change their Conversion Settings in order to have the same channel configuration.

  • Use different sounds sources.
