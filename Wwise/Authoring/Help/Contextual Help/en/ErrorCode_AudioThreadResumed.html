Audio thread resumed, audio restarts.

This message appears at various points during the game life cycle, such as when the game is put back in foreground (active state) by the OS or when a phone call ends on mobile devices. It is not an indication of a problem. It is most often the direct result of a call to AK::SoundEngine::WakeupFromSuspend. It can also be triggered automatically by Wwise on certain OS notifications. See also Audio thread resumed, audio restarts.
