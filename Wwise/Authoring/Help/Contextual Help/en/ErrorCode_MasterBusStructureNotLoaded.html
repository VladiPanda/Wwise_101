Output bus ... not found. Make sure that the Init bank is loaded first.

The output bus specified was not found in the structures loaded at that time. The object being loaded that refers to the bus is reported at the end of the error message.

Probable causes:

  • The Init bank was not loaded yet, or there was an error loading it.

  • The Init bank was not regenerated after a change to the bus structure in the project.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Regenerate your SoundBanks.

  • Make sure the Init.bnk is loaded through a successful call to AK::SoundEngine::LoadBank.
