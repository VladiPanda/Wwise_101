Nothing to play in Dynamic Sequence.

This error occurs when using AK::SoundEngine::DynamicSequence, if an invalid sound ID was enqueued. Usually preceded by a ResolveDialogueEvent notification with more information.

Probable causes:

  • AK::SoundEngine::DynamicDialogue::ResolveDialogueEvent failed to resolve an argument path to an actual sound.

  • Your banks may be out of date.

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Change the Dialogue Event paths and arguments in the AK::SoundEngine::DynamicDialogue::ResolveDialogueEventcall so they lead to a valid sound.

  • Regenerate and deploy your SoundBanks.
