Music system is stopped because a music playlist is modified.

This happens when the a Music Playlist or Container is modified while playing. This usually occurs when Wwise is connected to the game.

Probable causes:

  • The music structure was modified while it played.

  • The in-game music structure is different than the one in the Wwise project (old content).

Recommended resolution steps:

  • Regenerate your music SoundBanks and redeploy.
