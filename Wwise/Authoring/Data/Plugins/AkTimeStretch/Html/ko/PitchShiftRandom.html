Pitch Shift Random

Offsets the Pitch Shift amount specified by a random amount up to plus or minus the Pitch Shift random value. This will be effective for the whole duration of the voice on which the Pitch Shift will be applied. 재생 도중 전달 받은 RTPC는 다음 재생에만 고려됩니다.

단위: cents (센트)

Default value: 0
Range: 0 to 4800
